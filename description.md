## Full Description
*(Play Store — max 4000 characters)*

STK Camera is a mobile field assistant that brings laboratory precision to soil testing — entirely offline.

By using your camera to analyze pH and NPK color strips, the app provides an objective reading that confirms colors more accurately than the human eye. Once the chemical data is extracted, it generates a structured technical summary that you can copy to your clipboard and use with any external AI model for immediate fertilizer and crop recommendations.

**How it works:**
1. Run your soil test using any standard Soil Test Kit (STK)
2. Point your camera at the color result strip
3. Get instant pH, Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium readings
4. Copy the generated soil report and paste into any AI assistant for recommendations

**Key Features:**

🎨 CIEDE2000 Color Matching
Industry-standard color difference algorithm that reads pH and NPK strips with greater consistency than the human eye — even in varying field lighting conditions.

💬 Structured Prompt Generator
Automatically converts your soil chemical data into a clean, technical summary — ready to paste into ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI model for fertilizer and crop recommendations.

🎛️ Custom Color Calibration
Adjust the default reference colors to match your specific STK brand. Every kit is different — calibrate once, save locally, get accurate results every time. Free feature.

📊 Full NPK + pH Profile
Complete macro-nutrient analysis: pH level, Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), and Potassium (K) — the four core indicators for soil health and crop readiness.

🌍 Global Compatibility
Works with any standard soil test kit worldwide. No geographic restriction on the free version — usable by students and researchers in any country.

📶 Offline-First Design
Core color reading and analysis works without internet. Built for remote farms, research fields, and areas with poor or no signal.

📋 Report Export
Save a clean soil analysis report directly to your device gallery. Perfect for submitting to technicians, instructors, or keeping field records.

**Who is this for?**
- Agriculture and soil science students
- Farm technicians and agronomists
- DENR field officers
- Researchers conducting soil surveys
- Farmers who use standard soil test kits
